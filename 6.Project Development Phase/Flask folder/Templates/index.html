About Us

Strategic Product Placement Analysis is a data-driven approach used to optimize how products are positioned within a retail environment to maximize visibility, customer engagement, and sales performance. By analyzing purchasing patterns, customer movement behavior, and sales trends, businesses can identify high-impact shelf locations and strategically place key products where they are most likely to attract attention and drive conversions.

This analysis leverages sales data, profit margins, category performance, and customer preferences to determine which products should be positioned at eye level, near checkout counters, or in high-traffic areas. Heatmaps, clustering techniques, and trend analysis help uncover hidden patterns in customer behavior, enabling retailers to redesign store layouts for better efficiency and profitability.
